### Decision

**agentkan** wins on discoverability and theme: someone reading the name guesses
what it does (kanban where the AI agent and you share the board). Commands:
`agentkan` / `ak`.

jsonkan was the other top pick for pure discoverability. fring is the most
brandable if opacity is acceptable.
